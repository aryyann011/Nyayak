DOCUMENT_ID: MVA_2019
TOPIC: Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act, 2019
EMERGENCY RIGHTS:
- Golden Hour: The time period lasting one hour following a traumatic injury during which there is the highest likelihood of preventing death.
- Good Samaritan Law: Any person who provides emergency medical or non-medical care to a victim at the scene of an accident shall not be liable for any civil or criminal action.

PENALTIES:
- Drunk Driving: Fine up to Rs. 10,000 and/or 6 months imprisonment.
- Driving without License: Rs. 5,000.
- Over-speeding: Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 2,000 for LMV.
- Not wearing Seatbelt: Rs. 1,000.
