    Cybercrime and Legal Remedies

Cybercrime includes online fraud, hacking, identity theft, cyberbullying, and financial scams.

Common Types:
1. Online payment fraud.
2. Social media account hacking.
3. Phishing emails.
4. Cyber stalking.
5. Fake job scams.

What To Do:
- Immediately report to Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in).
- Contact bank to block fraudulent transactions.
- File complaint at cyber police station.
- Preserve screenshots and digital evidence.

Cybercrime complaints should be filed quickly to prevent further damage.

Police can trace digital transactions and investigate online fraud.
