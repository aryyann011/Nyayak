Consumer Complaint Process

Consumers have the right to file complaints against defective goods or poor services.

Grounds for Complaint:
1. Defective product.
2. Poor service.
3. Overcharging.
4. False advertising.
5. Unfair trade practices.

Steps to File Complaint:
1. Send written complaint to seller/service provider.
2. Keep bill and proof of purchase.
3. File complaint in Consumer Court (District Commission).
4. Submit documents and evidence.

Jurisdiction:
- District Commission: Up to specified claim limit.
- State Commission: Higher claim amount.
- National Commission: High-value claims.

Possible Remedies:
- Refund of money.
- Replacement of product.
- Compensation.
- Penalty against seller.

Consumers can also file complaint online through e-Daakhil portal.

Consumer Protection Act ensures fair trade practices.
